TRAP

One of the most fun games you'll ever play!

Trap shooting is probably the most familiar game to new shooters. It consists of a single trap and five shooting locations as shown in the diagram below. Trap is one of the easiest games to learn, but is difficult to master because the flight of the target varies due to the side-to-side motion of the trap.

For a detailed explanation of the game, please check out the Wikipedia Link on Trap Shooting

Trap Diagram

Players take 5 shots at each of the 5 stations on a trap field. A perfect score is 25/25!
